Jay pritchett Jay Pritchett is the patriarch of the Pritchett family, the father of Claire, Mitchell, and Joe, the husband of Gloria, and the stepfather of Manny. He is a Vietnam War veteran and the founder and former CEO of Pritchett's Closets & Blinds.
Manny Delgado Manny is portrayed as very mature and intuitive for his age, and he is frequently shown doing adult-like things, such as having deep emotional conversations with adults, drinking coffee/expresso, and dealing with problems like an adult, which always confuses his stepfather Jay.
Stella pritchett She was previously owned by Guillermo, a dog trainer who sought Jay's advice about his new business idea, which was harshly criticized. He couldn't keep her because he needed to return to school, so Jay's family decided to care for her.
Gloria Delgado Gloria Maria Ramirez Pritchett is the vivacious, carefree Colombian daughter of Pilar and Fulgencio, the wife of Jay Pritchett, and the mother of Manny Delgado and Joe Pritchett. She gains citizenship in the United States. She has worked as a hairdresser, taxi driver, spokesmodel, mover, philosophy professor, stay-at-home mom, and housewife before becoming a realtor.

Phil Dunphy Claire Dunphy's husband and father of their three children, Haley, Alex, and Luke, is Philip Humphrey "Phil" Dunphy. In California, he works as a real estate agent.Phil Dunphy was born on April 3, 1968 in Key West, Florida to Frank and Grace Dunphy. He is Australian on his mother's side. He lived near Okeechobee Lake. ("Legacy") He attended high school and was an active member of the cheerleading squad, a habit he maintained until his college years.
Claire Dunphy Claire Melinda Dunphy is Mitchell's older sister and the daughter of Jay and DeDe Pritchett. She is also Phil's wife and the mother of Haley, Alex, and Luke. Claire was a stay-at-home mom until the fifth season, when she began working at Pritchett's Closets & Blinds for her father. She eventually succeeded him as CEO of the company before leaving to become Senior VP of her favorite organizing chain store, Organize 'Em.
Hayley Dunphy She is the eldest son of Claire and Phil Dunphy, as well as the brother of Luke and Alex. Haley has had an on-again, off-again relationship with her ex-boyfriend, Dylan, and has now married him as her first husband, with twin children Paula "Poppy" and George.
Alex Dunphy Alexandra Anastasia "Alex" Dunphy is Claire and Phil Dunphy's middle child and second daughter. Alex is clearly the brightest and most accomplished of Claire and Phil's children. She has an older brother, Luke, and a younger sister, Haley..
Luke Dunphy Lucas Philip "Luke" Dunphy is Claire and Phil Dunphy's third and youngest, though tallest, child, as well as Haley and Alex Dunphy's younger brother. Luke is a rambunctious, playful, mischievous, and immature boy who is often doing his own thing. He is also known for his lack of intelligence..
